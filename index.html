How It Works

Step into a retro-hacker space where each Frog is hashed from your *wallet address + optional ID*. Our system maps that hash to ASCII frog attributes—species, skin color, accessories, and more.

  1. Connect Wallet: Link your Phantom wallet for a secure session.
  2. ID Input: Enter a unique ID or default to your wallet address alone.
  3. Hash Magic: We generate a 32-bit integer that determines your frog’s style.
  4. Frog Attributes: Everything from “skin color” to “croak level” is set by the hash.
  5. Unique Output: Same input, same frog. Totally yours!
